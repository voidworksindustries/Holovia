Privacy Policy

Holovia Privacy Policy (Extended Version)

Effective Date: 16/04/2026

Developed by: Voidworks Industries

1. Introduction

Voidworks Industries operates the mobile game Holovia.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use the Service. By using Holovia, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Holovia does not directly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.

However, the Service uses third-party tools that automatically collect certain information when you use the app.

This information may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID or device-specific IDs)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location (country or region)
  • Device information (model, operating system, language)
  • App usage data (interactions, session duration, gameplay events)
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data

Game progress, unlocks, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Holovia integrates third-party services that may collect and process data independently.

These include:

Advertising Services

  • Unity LevelPlay (IronSource Mediation)
  • Unity Ads
  • Google AdMob

These services may collect device identifiers, usage data, and approximate location to display and measure advertisements.

Analytics and Attribution Services

  • Tenjin
  • Firebase Analytics

These services collect usage data to understand installs, sessions, and player behavior.

Crash Reporting

  • Firebase Crashlytics

Crashlytics collects diagnostic information such as crash logs, device state, operating system version, and application state at the time of a crash.

Platform Services

  • Google Play Services

Used for app distribution, updates, and general platform functionality.

4. How Information Is Used

Information collected through third-party services is used to:

  • Provide, display, and optimize advertisements
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Analyze installs, sessions, and gameplay behavior
  • Monitor crashes and diagnose technical issues
  • Improve game performance, stability, and balance
  • Enhance the overall user experience

Voidworks Industries does not sell personal data.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), data processing is based on:

  • Your consent (for personalized advertising where required)
  • Legitimate interests (such as improving performance, preventing fraud, and maintaining service stability)

6. Data Sharing

Data may be shared with third-party service providers listed in this policy for the purposes described above.

These providers process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

7. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers.

Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective retention policies.

8. Data Security

We rely on third-party providers that implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under data protection laws, including:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to or restrict processing
  • The right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us. We will assist where possible or direct you to the relevant third-party provider.

10. Advertising Choices

Users may be presented with a choice between personalized and non-personalized ads where required by law.

  • Personalized ads use device identifiers and behavioral data
  • Non-personalized ads are based on general information such as region or device type

You can reset your advertising preferences through your device settings.

11. Children's Privacy

Holovia is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If such data is identified, it will be removed where possible.

12. International Data Transfers

Third-party services may process data on servers located outside your country of residence.

These transfers are handled in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

13. Third-Party Privacy Policies

For more information, please review the privacy policies of our partners:

  • Unity
  • IronSource (LevelPlay)
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services
  • Tenjin
  • Firebase

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Changes will be posted on our website with an updated effective date.
