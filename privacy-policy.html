Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Account information (username, email, password)
  • Profile data (avatar, preferences, settings)
  • Gameplay data (achievements, statistics, progress)
  • Device information (OS, hardware specs)
  • Usage analytics (game events, interaction patterns)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Personalize your gaming experience
  • Maintain account security
  • Send service updates and announcements
  • Analyze trends and optimize performance
  • Detect and prevent fraud

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure servers with multiple firewalls
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Two-factor authentication options
  • Data backup and disaster recovery systems

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent. We may share aggregated, anonymized data for research and analytics purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Download your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings. We respect Do Not Track signals.

7. Contact Us

If you have privacy concerns or questions, contact us at privacy@voidworks.com

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes take effect upon posting. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
