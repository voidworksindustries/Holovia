Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download the game?

Visit our Download page and select your platform. Follow the installation instructions for your operating system.

What are the minimum system requirements?

Check the Technical Specifications section on the Voidworks product page for detailed requirements.

How do I create an account?

Click "Download" and follow the in-game setup wizard. You'll be prompted to create an account with your email address.

I'm experiencing performance issues. What should I do?

Try the following: Lower graphics settings, close background applications, update your GPU drivers, and verify your game files through the launcher.

How often are updates released?

We release major updates monthly and hotfixes as needed. Check our Patch Notes for the latest changes.

Can I play on multiple devices?

Yes! With cloud save support, your progress syncs across all platforms automatically.

How do I report a bug?

Use the in-game bug report feature or contact our support team at support@voidworks.com

Is there a multiplayer community?

Absolutely! Join our Discord server to connect with thousands of players worldwide.
